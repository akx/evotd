EvoTD – by @akx / akx for Ludum Dare 24.
Started on Sunday August 26, 10:12 GMT and released roughly 9 hours later.
Works on modern Chromes and Firefoxes.
Source. Full locally playable download w/ source.

Some protips:
  • Your goal: Don't let enemies (they spawn in the spawner on the left) get to your Core (the green thing on the right). Do this by placing towers to stop them.
  • The enemy also evolves.
  • It's usually not a good idea to sell your towers.
  • Mutations may happen even when [C]loning your towers.
  • The enemy really rages if it can't find a route to your core.
  • This game makes no guarantees of even trying to be fair. Remember: Dying is fun. And who knows, maybe next time your starting gene (tower) pool will be more awesome.
  • A "?" on a tower means it hasn't acquired an enemy. A "R" means it's busy reloading.
  • You may also hit A to pick up the first tower in your tower tray for placement.
